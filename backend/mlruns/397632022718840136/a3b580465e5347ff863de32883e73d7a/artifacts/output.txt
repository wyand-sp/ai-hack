
Bulgaria is a beautiful country with many places to visit. Some popular tourist destinations include the capital city Sofia, the historic town of Plovdiv, the Black Sea coast, and the picturesque Rila Mountains. It ultimately depends on your interests and preferences. If you enjoy nature and hiking, the Rila Mountains would be a great option. If you prefer city life, Sofia and Plovdiv offer a mix of history, culture, and modern amenities. The Black Sea coast is perfect for beach lovers and offers a variety of resorts and activities.
